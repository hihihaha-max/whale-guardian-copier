## Short Description (150 words — for form)

Whale Guardian Copier is an AI trading agent built on OpenClaw that automatically detects large whale transactions (>$500K), runs a full 5-step safety analysis using 11 Binance Skills, and executes copy trades in under 0.1 seconds.

When a whale buys a token, the agent instantly: analyzes the wallet (Address Info Skill), checks token health (Token Info Skill), audits the smart contract for rug pulls and honeypots (Contract Audit Skill), generates a trading signal (Signal Skill), then flash-executes a 0.5% position (Spot Skill) — all with one click.

The agent supports 6 trading venues: Binance CEX, BSC (PancakeSwap), Ethereum (Uniswap), Solana (Jupiter), Base (Aerodrome), and Pump.fun. It includes configurable Take Profit / Stop Loss, a 30-day PNL dashboard, auto-generated Binance Square posts, and full tri-language support (VI/EN/ZH).

Built with Claude Sonnet 4 + all 11 official Binance Skills.

## What problem does it solve?

Retail traders miss whale trades because:
1. They lack real-time on-chain monitoring tools
2. They can't audit smart contracts for safety quickly
3. By the time they react manually, the opportunity is gone

Whale Guardian Copier solves all 3 — automatically, safely, in 0.1 seconds.

## What makes it unique?

1. **Safety-first copy trading** — every whale trade passes a 7-point contract audit before execution. If risk score > 30, the trade is automatically rejected and a warning is posted to Square.

2. **Full workflow automation** — from whale detection to trade execution to Binance Square post, zero manual steps required.

3. **Multi-chain in one UI** — CEX and 5 DEX chains in a single interface, with smart slippage defaults per chain.

4. **Viral by design** — auto-generates Binance Square posts after every trade, creating organic social proof and community growth.

5. **Genuinely educational** — Claude AI explains every decision in plain language, helping users understand *why* a trade was taken or rejected.
